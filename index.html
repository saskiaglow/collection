Projects by Saskia Globig for the 2023 class of "Software for People" at Yale School of Art.


1.
A webzine collecting three articles on Medieval European fashion and ornament, looking at why they're so appealing to people again right now. Three different editions of the project are available in the site's archive.
2.
A collaboration with Kevin Chen displayed at EIK Gallery on Yale's Campus during Open Studios. The imagery changes depending on your answers to a survey, and a receipt would automatically print, giving you a personalized reading. You can take the survey here.
3.
A gift for my boyfriend, protected by location-sensitive instructions and a puzzle. I collected audio recordings from our friends in Philadelphia and interspersed them with my field recordings from around the city.
